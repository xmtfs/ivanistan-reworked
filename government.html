GOVERNMENT

Our government is divided into several different parts which include:
1)Parliament witch holds the legislative powers in the republic.
The parliament hold 40 people which are appointed by the president and on its head is the president.
2)Cabinet of ministers witch has 9 ministries witch include:
a.Ministry of defence,
b.Ministry of foreign relations,
c.Ministry economy and agriculture,
d.Ministry of infrastructure and traffic,
e.Ministry of taxation,
f.Ministry of labor,social standards,
g.Ministry of internal affairs,
h.Ministry of education and general standards,
i.Ministry of law and country building.
3.Supreme court which holds judicial powers in the republic and has tree people representing it which are the president and 2 people appointed from the parliament.
