Government Agencies

Our government has many agencies that make the country work here is the list:
National bank our national bank is used for the regulation of our currency and our banking system and the main controller of credit cards.
The National Association for tech development and research is made for the development of our nation.
The Police is the main force for keeping security and order in our nation.
The Army is the force whitch guards the territory of our nation from external attack.
The Mail service is for delivering official and unofficial mail to our people.
